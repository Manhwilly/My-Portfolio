About Me

Hello! I'm Manh. My journey into the world of data began with a BSc in Data Science from Bournemouth University, where I developed a passion for uncovering the stories hidden within numbers. This led me to a strong skill set in statistical analysis, machine learning, and data visualisation.

However, I soon realised that data is most powerful when it's accessible. This drove me to learn **app development with Kotlin and React**. To complete my skillset, I studied **International Business Administration and Management at the University of Strathclyde**, ensuring I understand the strategic context behind the data.

Now, I thrive on the challenge of the full data lifecycle: from strategic analysis to building the final user-facing application. My goal is always to create value and solve meaningful problems.
